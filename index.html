James R. Daniels, CPA

Managing Partner

James brings 34 years of CPA licensing history across California, Nevada, and Arizona, A-clear license status for assurance engagement capability, successful peer review experience, and a career that spans public accounting, commercial banking, construction operations, real estate, and tax controversy.

His construction background includes steel fabrication and self-performing underground, grading, concrete, steel erection, drywall, and metal stud framing. That matters because contractor accounting is not theoretical: job costing, labor burden, equipment, cash flow, change orders, bonding, and project risk all show up in the numbers.

Juan Reyes, CPA

Partner | Books To Scale Corporation

Juan Reyes is a CPA and the founder and owner of Books To Scale Corporation, a Sacramento-based accounting and consulting business. He brings more than 15 years of accounting, tax, and consulting experience across public and private accounting, including work with agriculture, construction, and other closely held businesses.

After beginning his career as a private equity intern, Juan progressed through every level of the accounting vertical, developing a broad understanding of financial operations and long-term business strategy. He has been building his own practice since 2019 and earned his real estate license in 2025, adding firsthand transaction experience to the practical guidance he provides clients.
