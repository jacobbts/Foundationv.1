How it works

A simple path from login to resolution.

The workflow is intentionally straightforward: get into the right system, move files securely, and keep the right Foundation team connected to the issue.

  1. Choose your access point

    Use the client portal for document workflows or your hosted server login for assigned accounting system access.

  2. Work inside a consistent accounting environment

    Hosted access keeps the accounting system experience consistent for teams that need dependable desktop workflows.

  3. Send files through the secure workflow

    Upload tax documents, statements, payroll files, notices, or close support items without scattering details across email threads.

  4. Keep close work moving

    Share support items, cleanup questions, and monthly reporting details in one place so follow-up is easier to track.

  5. Route questions to the right team

    Accounting, payroll, tax, assurance, and advisory questions can be coordinated with the Foundation team handling your work.

  6. Coordinate support with Foundation

    If access, files, or systems need attention, Foundation can help diagnose the issue and keep the next step clear.

FAQs

Common client platform questions.

Quick answers for access, files, hosted accounting systems, and support requests.

Where do I log in?

Use the buttons at the top of this page for the client portal or hosted server. If your team has a separate assigned link, Foundation can confirm the right access point.

What should I upload through the portal?

Tax documents, notices, statements, payroll files, month-end support, and other sensitive records should move through the secure document workflow when possible.

Who is hosted server access for?

Hosted access is for clients using Foundation-supported accounting environments such as Sage 100 Contractor or QuickBooks Desktop workflows.

What if my login is not working?

Contact Foundation and include which access point you are using, the issue you are seeing, and whether it affects one user or the full team.

Can Foundation help with accounting workflow questions?

Yes. The platform is meant to support accounting access, file movement, close support, notices, and coordination with your Foundation advisory team.
